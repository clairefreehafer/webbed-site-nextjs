#### ingredients

- 100 grams avocado oil or any neutral flavored oil
- 65 grams tahini
- 60 grams soy sauce
- 60 grams rice vinegar
- 50 grams gochujang
- 20 grams honey or agave or brown rice syrup
- 60 grams water
- 1 clove garlic

#### instructions

1. add all the ingredients to a measuring jug or large jar.

2. blend with an immersion blender until smooth.
