#### ingredients

- 1 c vanilla ice cream
- 1.5 oz irish cream
- 1.5 oz coffee liqueur
- 0.75 oz vanilla vodka
- 1 oz half-and-half
- chocolate syrup
- whipped cream
- dark chocolate

#### instructions

1. add **vanilla ice cream**, **irish cream**, **coffee liqueur**, **vodka**, **half-and-half**, a pinch of **sea salt**, and 1 c of **ice** to a blender. blend.

1. add **chocolate syrup** around the side of a hurricane glass. pour in drink.

1. top with **whipped cream** and shaved **dark chocolate**.
