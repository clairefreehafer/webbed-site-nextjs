#### ingredients

- 1.5 oz jägermeister
- 3/4 oz orange liqueur (cointreau recommended)
- 3/4 oz lime juice
- 1/2 oz 1:1 simple syrup

#### instructions

1. combine ingredients in shaker and add ice. shake.

2. double strain into a coupe glass.

3. garnish with a lime wheel.
