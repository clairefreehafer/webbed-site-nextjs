#### ingredients

- 1.5 oz mezcal
- 3/4 oz orgeat
- 3/4 oz fresh-squeezed lime juice
- 3 dashes tobasco sauce
- 4-5 dashes angostura bitters
- mint
- nutmeg

#### instructions

1. in a shaker, combine **mezcal**, **orgeat**, **lime juice**, and **tobasco**.

1. fill shaker with crushed ice about halfway.

1. shake for about ⏲️ 5 seconds.

1. open pour into chilled glass, leaving room at the top

1. add **bitters**.

1. garnish with a sprit of **mint** and grated **nutmeg**.
