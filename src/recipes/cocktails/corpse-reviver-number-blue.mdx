#### ingredients

- 1 oz gin
- 1 oz cocchi americano
- 1 oz blue curaçao
- 1 oz lemon juice
- spritz of absinthe

#### instructions

1. spritz the bottom of your glass with **absinthe**.

1. add **gin**, **cocchi americano**, **blue coraçao**, and **lemon juice** with ice in a shaker. shake.

1. garnish with lemon peel
