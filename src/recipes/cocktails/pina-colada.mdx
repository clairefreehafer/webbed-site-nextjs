#### ingredients

- 1.5 oz white rum
- 1 oz overproof rum
- 2 oz cream of coconut
- 3 oz pineapple juice
- 1 c frozen pineapple
- 1 oz lime juice
- 0.5 oz green chartreuse
- 2 dashes coffee bitters
- nutmeg

#### instructions

1. combine **rums**, **cream of coconut**, **pineapple juice**, **pineapple**, **lime juice**, and **green chartreuse** in a blender. blend until smooth.

1. pour into glass. top with 2 dashes of **coffee bitters** and some grated **nutmeg**.
