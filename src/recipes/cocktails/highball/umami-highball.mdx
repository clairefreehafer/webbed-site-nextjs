#### ingredients

- 2 oz baldoria vermouth dry umami
- 2 oz soda water
- 1/6 oz white wine vinegar
- a pinch of salt
- 3 dried apricots

#### instructions

1. pour the **dry vermouth**, **white wine vinegar**, and a pinch of **salt** into a high ball with ice and top off with 2 oz of **soda water**.

2. stir gently.
3. garnish with three skewered **dried apricots**.
