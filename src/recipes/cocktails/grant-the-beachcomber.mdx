#### ingredients

- 1 oz jamaican spiced rum
- 1/2 oz smith and cross rum
- 3/4 oz macadamia nut liqueur
- 1 oz pineapple amaro
- orange

#### instructions

1. combine ingredients. add ice and stir.

1. add a big ice cube to a rocks glass. strain mixture into glass and garnish with an orange twist.
