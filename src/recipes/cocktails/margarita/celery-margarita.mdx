#### ingredients

- 1.5 oz blanco tequila
- 1 oz celery juice
- 3/4 oz lime juice
- 1/2 oz 1:1 simple syrup
- old bay

#### instructions

1. rim a coupe glass with lime juice and cover with **old bay**.

1. add **tequila**, **celery juice**, **lime juice**, and **simple syrup** to a cocktail shaker with ice. shake.

1. double strain into glass and garnish with a lime wheel.
