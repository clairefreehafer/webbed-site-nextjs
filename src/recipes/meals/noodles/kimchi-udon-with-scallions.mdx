#### ingredients

- 1 lb. udon noodles
- 1 cup finely chopped kimchi
- ⅓ cup kimchi juice
- 2 tbsp. gochujang
- ½ cup low-sodium chicken broth
- 4 large egg yolks, room temperature
- 3 scallions thinly sliced on a diagonal
- 5 tbsp. unsalted butter
- toasted sesame seeds

#### instructions

1. cook **udon** according to package directions.

2. heat **2 tbsp. unsalted butter** in a large skillet over medium-high. add **1 cup finely chopped kimchi** and **2 tbsp. gochujang** and cook, stirring occasionally, until kimchi is softened and lightly caramelized, about 4 minutes. Add **½ cup low-sodium chicken broth** and **⅓ cup kimchi juice** and bring to a simmer. cook until liquid is slightly reduced, about 3 minutes.

3. using tongs, transfer noodles to skillet and add remaining **3 tbsp. unsalted butter**; cook, tossing often, until sauce coats noodles, about 2 minutes. season with salt, if needed. divide among bowls and top with **egg yolks**, **scallions**, and **toasted sesame seeds**.
