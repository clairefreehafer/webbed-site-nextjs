#### variants

<details>
  <summary>creamy mushroom</summary>

1. heat a large drizzle of **olive oil** in a large pan over medium-high heat. add sliced **mushrooms**; season with **salt** and **pepper**. cook, stirring occasionally, until browned and slightly crispy, 5-7 minutes. turn off heat; transfer to a paper-towel-lined plate.

2. melt 6 tbsp plain **butter** in pan used for mushrooms over medium-high heat. add sliced **scallion whites** and cook until just softened, 1 minute. add 3tbsp **flour** and cook, stirring, until lightly browned, 1-2 minutes. whisk in 20oz **milk** and 1⁄2 cup reserved **pasta cooking water**, breaking up any flour clumps. simmer until slightly thickened, 3-4 minutes.

3. stir **cream cheese** into pan with sauce until melted and combined. stir in mushrooms, drained pasta, and **garlic herb butter**. season with **salt** and **pepper**. if needed, stir in more reserved cooking water a splash at a time until pasta is coated in a creamy sauce.

</details>
