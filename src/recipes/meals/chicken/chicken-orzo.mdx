#### ingredients

- 24 oz chicken breasts
- 6 oz orzo pasta
- 2 zucchinis, 🔪 sliced into ¼-in-thick rounds
- 2 roma tomatoes, 🔪 cut into ½-in-thick wedges
- 8 oz mozzarella, 🔪 cubed ½ in
- 1 lemon, 🔪 halved
- ¼ oz parsley, 🔪 rougly chopped
- 2 tbsp italian seasoning
- ½ c panko breadcrumbs
- ½ c parmesan cheese, shredded

#### instructions

1. adjust rack to upper position and preheat oven to 🔥 425 degrees.

1. bring a large pot of salted water to a 🔥 boil.

1. toss **zucchini** and **tomato** with 1 tbsp **olive oil** and half the **italian seasoning** on a baking sheet. season with **salt** and **pepper**.

1. roast zucchini and tomato in oven until just shy of tender, about ⏲️ 10 minutes.

1. heat broiler to 🔥 high or increase oven temperature to 🔥 500 degrees.

1. sprinkle veggies with **panko**, **mozzarella**, and **parmesan**.

1. broil (or bake) until panko is golden brown, cheese is melted, and veggies are tender, ⏲️ 3-5 minutes.

1. once water is boiling, add the **orzo**. cook, stirring occasionally, until al dente, ⏲️ 9-11 minutes. drain, then return to pot.

1. with your hand on top of one **chicken breast**, cut ¾ of the way through middle, parallel to cutting board, stopping before you slice through completely. repeat with other chicken breast. open each up and season all over with **salt**, **pepper**, and remaining **italian seasoning**.

1. heat a drizzle of **olive oil** in a large pan over 🔥 medium-high heat. add chicken and cook until no longer pink in center, ⏲️ 3-4 minutes per side. remove from pan and set aside on a plate.

1. add **juice** from one lemon half and half the **parsley** to pot with orzo and toss to combine. season to taste with **salt** and **pepper**. divide orzo between plates. top with veggies and chicken. drizzle with any chicken juices from plate and a squeeze of lemon. garnish with remaining parsley and serve.
