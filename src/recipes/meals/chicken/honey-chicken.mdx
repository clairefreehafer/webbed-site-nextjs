#### ingredients

- 1 c jasmine rice
- 24 oz chicken breasts
- 8 oz snow peas, 🔪 trimmed
- 2 tbsp ginger, 🔪 minced
- 4 cloves garlic, 🔪 minced
- 1 lime, 🔪 cut into wedges
- 3 tbsp white wine vinegar
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 stock concentrate(?)
- 8 tsp honey

#### instructions

1. set **rice** to cook.

1. season **chicken** all over with **salt** and **pepper**.

1. heat a drizzle of **oil** in a large pan over 🔥 medium-high heat. add **chicken** to pan and cook until browned and no longer pink in center, ⏲️ 4-7 minutes per side. remove from pan and set aside.

1. heat a drizzle of **oil** in same pan over 🔥 medium-low heat. add **ginger** and **garlic**. cook, tossing, until soft and fragrant, about ⏲️ 1 minute. pour in **white wine vinegar** and let reduce until almost dry and evaporated.

1. stir **soy sauce**, 2 tbsp **water**, **stock concentrate**, and **honey** into same pan. let simmer until mixture thickens to a glaze-like consistency, ⏲️ 2-3 minutes. remove from heat, then return **chicken** to pan and toss to coat. set aside until rest of meal is ready.

1. heat a drizzle of **oil** in a medium pan over 🔥 medium heat. add **snow peas** and cook, tossing, until tender but still a little crisp, ⏲️ 3-4 minutes. season with **salt**, **pepper**, and a squeeze of **lime**.

1. divide rice between plates. top with snow peas and chicken. serve with lime wedges on the side for squeezing over.
