#### contents

<details>
  <summary>radish, tomato, & onion</summary>

in a medium bowl, combine xdiced **radishes**, diced **roma tomato**, finely chopped **cilantro**, minced **yellow onion**, a squeeze of **lime juice**, and a large drizzle of **olive oil**. season generously with **salt** and **pepper**.

</details>

<details>
  <summary>sweet potato & onion</summary>

adjust rack to top position and preheat oven to 450 degrees. toss diced **sweet potato** on a baking sheet with a drizzle of **oil**, **salt**, and **pepper**. roast on top rack, tossing halfway through, until browned and tender, 20-25 minutes. remove roasted sweet potato from sheet.

heat a drizzle of **oil** in a large pan over medium-high heat. add diced **yellow onion**; season with **salt** and **pepper**. cook, stirring, until tender, 5-7 minutes. once sweet potato is done roasting, add to pan and season. cook until fragrant, 1 minute. turn off heat.

</details>
