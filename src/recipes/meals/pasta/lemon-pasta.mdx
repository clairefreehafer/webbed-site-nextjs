#### ingredients

- Â½ pound fresh or dried linguine
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon freshly grated lemon zest, plus more for serving
- 4 tablespoons heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons freshly grated parmesan cheese, plus extra cheese to serve on the side

#### instructions

1. cook **linguine** according to package directions. drain.

1. heat the **butter** in a skillet and add the **lemon zest**.

1. add the **heavy cream** to the butter and lemon zest mixture. add the pasta and **lemon juice** and stir until just heated through. add the **parmesan** and toss.

1. serve with additional **parmesan** and **lemon zest** on the side.
