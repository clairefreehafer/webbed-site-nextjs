#### ingredients

- 4 flatbreads
- 20 oz pulled pork
- 2 roma tomatoes, diced
- 4 scallions, thinly sliced
- 2 tbsp bbq seasoning
- 1 c mozzarella cheese, shredded
- 4 oz bbq sauce
- 1 tbsp butter

#### instructions

1. adjust broiler rack so that it is in position closest to flame and place a baking sheet on rack. preheat broiler to high.

2. heat **butter** and 1 tbsp **olive oil** in a large pan over medium-high heat. stir in **bbq seasoning**. cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.

3. add **pulled pork** and 1/4 cup **water** to pan, stirring to coat. cook, stirring occasionally, until warmed through and water is mostly evaporated but still a little saucy, 2-3 minutes. season with **salt** and **pepper**.

4. scatter **pork** evenly over **flatbreads**, then repeat with **tomato**, **scallion whites**, and **mozzarella**. carefully remove baking sheet from broiler and sprinkle with a drizzle of **olive oil**. transfer flatbreads to sheet and sprinkle each with a large drizzle of **olive oil**.

5. return baking sheet to broiler and broil until cheese melts and flatbreads begin to turn golden brown and crips, 3-4 minutes.

6. drizzle **bbq sauce** over flatbreads and sprinkle with **scallion greens**. cut into slices and serve.
