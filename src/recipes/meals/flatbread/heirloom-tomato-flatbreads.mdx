#### ingredients

- 4 flatbreads
- 8 oz heirloom grape tomatoes
  - halved lengthwise
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella
  - thinly sliced
- 4 oz pesto
- 5 tsp balsamic vinegar
- 4 oz arugula
- 1 oz basil
- 2 oz walnuts
  - roughly chopped
- 4 tsp olive oil

1. place **flatbreads** on a baking sheet. spread **pesto** over top of flatbreads in an even layer.

1. top flatbreads with **mozzarella** and **tomatoes**. bake in oven until cheese is melty and tomatoes are softened, ‚è≤Ô∏è 8-10 minutes.

1. meanwhile, in a medium bowl, whisk together 1 tbsp **vinegar** and a large drizzle of **olive oil**.

1. add **arugula** to bowl with dressing and toss to combine. season with **salt** and **pepper**.

1. top baked flatbreads with salad and sprinkle with **walnuts**. tear **basil leaves** from stems and scatter over top. cut into slices and serve.
