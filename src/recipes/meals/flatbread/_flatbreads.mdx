#### toppings

- arugula
- carmelized onions

<details>
  <summary>mushrooms & shallot</summary>

heat a large drizzle of **olive oil** in a large pan over medium-high heat. add **mushrooms** and **shallot**, then season. cook, stirring, until browned and softened, 8-10 minutes. season with **salt** and **pepper**; remove pan from heat.

top baked flatbreads with truffle oil.

</details>
<details>
  <summary>pineapple & red onion</summary>

drain **pineapple** over a small bowl, reserving **juice**. stir Â¼ of the **onion**, ~2.5 tsp **red wine vinegar**, and a pinch of **salt** into bowl with juice. set aside.

heat a drizzle of **oil** in a large pan over medium heat. add remaining **onion** and cook, stirring and adding splashes of **water** as necessary to prevent sticking, until softened, 6-8 minutes.

once cooked onion is softened, increase heat under pan to medium high. add **pineapple** and another drizzle of **oil**; cook, stirring, until pineapple is lightly browned, 2-3 minutes. stir in remaining **vinegar**. cook, stirring, until onion and pineapple are caramelized and jammy, 1-2 minutes more. turn off heat and season with **salt** and **pepper**.

top baked flatbreads with the pickled onions.

</details>
<details>
  <summary>grape tomato</summary>

in a small bowl, combine halved **grape tomatoes**, **garlic**, and a drizzle of **olive oil**. season with **salt** and **pepper**.

</details>

- spinach

<details>
  <summary>yellow squash & scallions</summary>

heat a drizzle of **olive oil** in a pan over medium-high heat. add thinly sliced **scallions** and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. add **yellow squash** and cook until soft and lightly browned, 5-6 minutes, tossing. season with **salt** and **pepper**.

</details>
<details>
  <summary>zucchini</summary>

heat a drizzle of **olive oil** in a large pan over medium-high heat. add **zucchini** and cook, stirring, until lightly browned and softened, 5-6 minutes. season with **salt** and **pepper**. turn off heat.

</details>
